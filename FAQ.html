Frequently Asked Questions

  • Where do you source your fabric?
    • Jersey items are sourced within the UK although some fabrics may be from designers in America or in Europe, while at times there can be a UK designer and this will be stated in the title. Linen for bonnets is either sourced from UK sellers but tends to arrive from Germany and is of a wonderful quality.
  • How do I care for my items?
    • We suggest that items are hand washed and laid flat to dry unless it is stated in the listing otherwise. This way you can extend the life of your garment as long as possible.
  • Will you be restocking an item once it has sold out?
    • Sometimes items can be seasonal therefore you may not be able to find a certain style of bonnet during the winter seasons if it was designed for summer, however we try to keep a varied style available and we are more than welcome to take custom orders. Some types of fabric can be limited and so only a certain amount of bonnets will be made.
  • How long will it take for me to receive my item?
    • At the minute everything is made to order and therefore requires a 1-2 week turn around. We are keeping our fingers crossed to provide ready-made items in the future.
  • How much is shipping?
    • Shipping prices will vary depending on where you live, we do sell items across the world so please check the listing for shipping costs.
  • What is your return/refund policy?
    • Currently as all items are custom made we do not accept returns unless there is a fault with the item you have ordered.
Any other questions please feel free to contact us on our email lavinia@fromacorntooak.co.uk
